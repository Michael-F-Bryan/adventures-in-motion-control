[−][src]Module uom::si::specific_heat_capacity

Specific heat capacity (base unit joule per kilogram kelvin, m² · s⁻² · K⁻¹).

This quantity might be used to define the heat capacity of a material. To define heat capacity of an object, use heat capacity.

Specific heat capacity has the same kind as temperature interval, as this quantity relates to change of temperature. Not of kind TemperatureKind, used by thermodynamic temperature. See thermodynamic temperature for a full explanation.
